Adas Polo

Description

Adas polo is a traditional Persian lentil rice.

This dish is usually eaten with meat but can also be veganized.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2-3 garlic cloves
  • 2 cups of green lentils
  • 3 cups of rice
  • Turmeric, curry, cumin, salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: diced carrots
  • Sunflower oil or olive oil

Steps

  1. Put the oil in a big pan to heat
  2. Cut the onions into small dices and press the garlic cloves into small bits and add both to the pan until golden
  3. Wash the lentils several times and add it to the pan.
  4. Add the spices and salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Add 3-4 cups of water to the lentils and let them cook for about 20 minutes.
  6. Wash your rice several times.
  7. Add the rice to the lentils after 20 minutes.
  8. Optional: you can add some carrots to taste.
  9. Stir everything thouroughly and let cook for 20-25 minutes
