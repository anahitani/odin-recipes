Oven-Baked Potatoes

Description

This very simple but delicious recipe for oven-baked potatoes will make you addicted to them!

It's is simple yet tastes heavenly and you only need a few ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 1kg potatoes
  • 2 cloves of garlic or one table spoon of garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • One tsp of provincial herbs
  • Sunflower oil or olive oil

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 180 degrees Celcius.
  2. Wash the potatoes and cut them into 4 pieces, leave the skin!
  3. Add the potatoes on a baking tray
  4. Press the garlic cloves and add them to the potatoes or add garlic powder
  5. Poor over 3-4 table spoons of olive oil
  6. Add salt, pepper and herbs
  7. Mix everything well and distribute the potatoes evenly on the baking tray
  8. Put the tray in the oven and let it bake for 25-30 minutes until golden
  9. Enjoy!
