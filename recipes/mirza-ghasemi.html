Mirza Ghasemi

Description

Mirza Ghasemi is a traditional Persian dish, which is either eaten with rice or bread.

This dish is usually eaten as a starter but because of its heavy consistency, especially when eaten with rice, many people also eat it as a main dish. The main ingredients involve lots of garlic, barbecues aubergines and eggs.

Ingredients

  • Looots of garlic cloves, about 6-7!
  • Around 200g of tomato paste
  • 3-4 tomatoes
  • One kg of barbecues aubergines
  • 3 to 4 eggs
  • 2 tbsp of turmeric
  • Sunflower oil or olive oil
  • 400g of rice or bread

Steps

  1. Put the oil in a big pan to heat
  2. Press the garlic cloves into small bits and add it to the pan until golden
  3. Add the tomato paste to the garlic
  4. Slice the tomatoes into smaller pieces and add it to the pan
  5. Let everything cook for around 2-3 minutes
  6. Then add the barbecued aubergines
  7. Put salt and pepper to taste and add the turmeric
  8. Stir everything thouroughly and let simmer for 20-25 minutes
  9. In the meantime cook your rice
